NTU secured best project Award at PASTIC UAF ICT EXPO-19

Some people dream of success, while other people get up every morning and make it happen.‚Äù Aiman Nadeem

One-day PASTIC-UAF Information, Communications and Technology (ICT) Exhibition was held on February 20,2019 at the Expo Center, University of Agriculture Faisalabad.

Congratulations to Muhammad Usman Aslam, Zain-ul-Abidin and Aiman Nadeem (techtrendz team) from DCS, NTU, for winning the Best Project Award in PASTIC-UAF ICT EXPO 2019.

The title of winning best project award is 'Analysis of Emerging Technology Trends Around the Globe: 2014 - 2018', supervised by Dr. Sohail Jabbar, Assistant Professor at DCS, and Director of Graduate Studies, NTU. He was Post-Doctoral Researcher at Kyungpook National University, Daegu, South Korea.
