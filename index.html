Analysis of Emerging

Technology Trends
around the Globe: 2014 - 18

techtrendz analyzes the evolution of technology trends around the globe from 2014 to 2018, using as input the predictions made in the Horizon Reports, MIT Technology Review And Predictions made by IEEE on Higher Education. Horizon Report is published yearly since 2004. Each edition attempts to forecast the most promising technologies likely to impact along three horizons: the short term (the year of the report), the midterm (the following two years), and the long term (the following four years)
